Japan
Population: 125.8 million (2024 est.)
Random fact: Japan has over 6,800 islands!
⚡ History flash: During the Sakoku period (1635–1853), Japan was almost completely isolated from the outside world for over 200 years — only Dutch and Chinese ships were permitted to trade in Nagasaki. This era forged a unique cultural identity.
🇧🇷
Brazil
Population: 216.4 million
Random fact: The Amazon rainforest covers ~60% of Brazil, producing 20% of world's oxygen.
📜 Insane history: Brazil was the only South American country to host the Portuguese monarchy! In 1808, King Dom João VI fled Napoleon and moved the entire royal court to Rio de Janeiro, making it the capital of the Portuguese Empire for 13 years.
🇪🇬
Egypt
Population: 112.7 million
Random fact: Ancient Egyptians used hieroglyphs for over 3,500 years.
🏺 History deep dive: Cleopatra VII lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid. Egypt’s civilization spans 5,000+ years — making it one of the longest continuous histories on Earth.
🇫🇷
France
Population: 68.4 million
Random fact: The Louvre Museum has 380,000+ objects, but only ~35,000 on display.
👑 Revolutionary history: The French Revolution (1789) not only changed France but gave birth to modern political ideologies: "Liberté, égalité, fraternité". The storming of the Bastille is celebrated worldwide as a symbol of uprising.
